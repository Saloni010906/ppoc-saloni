Public Policy and Opinion Cell

Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur

Mission of the cell

The Public Policy and Opinion Cell focused on developing a well-informed student body by fostering in detailed understanding of public policy and socio-political issues.

The main motive of the cell is to engage in constructive discourse and give meaningful contributions to policy dialogue in India.

Vision of the cell

To be a premier and flexible platform at IIT Kanpur that nurtures informed leadership and civic responsibility by bridging the gap between technical education and public policy awareness. Also to proclaim the interactive sessions in a profound manner.

Activities organised by the cell

Organizing interactive sessions with panel discussions with eminent policymakers, UPSC Seminars by scholars and experts, and public intellectuals.

Hosting debates, workshops, Competitions in Epoch Nexus like RAJNEETI , VIDESH NEETI , ECO-CRAFT of Policy Conclave.

Publishing opinion pieces, posters, blogs, and newsletters to promote informed expression and diverse viewpoints among students. Also to conduct policy research surveys.

Collaborating with think tanks like Model United Nations with other IITs, academic institutions & NGOs for internships and and different opportunities which helps to pave a better career path.
